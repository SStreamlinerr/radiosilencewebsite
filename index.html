Welcome

Radio Silence is a 5-piece band comprised of:
Lottie Bastock (vocals), Struan McKenzie (lead guitar), Jay Swann (rhythm guitar), Flora Woods (bass guitar), and Ben Mangen (drums).
They play a large range of genres from grunge to jazz, and artists such as Nirvana, Muse, and Blur.
