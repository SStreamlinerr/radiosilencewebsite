Radio Silence is a 5-piece rock band from the Scottish Borders comprised of:

Struan McKenzie Lead Guitar
Lottie Bastock Vocals
Jay Swann Rhythm Guitar
Flora Woods Bass Guitar
Ben Mangen Drums

They play a large range of genres from grunge, punk, rock, even jazz, and artists such as Nirvana, Muse, Blur, and more.

So far they have written two of their own songs: Black and Blue, and No Way. Unfortunately they haven't been released yet but they are played at most gigs.
